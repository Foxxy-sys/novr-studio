Bean & Brew

Client Bean & Brew Coffee Co.
Category Coffee Shop — Website
Year 2026
Scope Design, Frontend Development
Challenge

A warm brand, lost online.

Bean & Brew had a loyal following in-store, but their online presence undersold the experience. The existing site was templated, slow, and gave visitors no reason to choose them over a chain coffee shop down the street.

The brief was simple to state and hard to execute: make the website feel as warm, considered, and specific as the physical space — without slowing it down.

Solution

Cream, brown, and restraint.

We built a single-page experience anchored around a cream-and-brown palette, generous whitespace, and a strong editorial type pairing. Every section earns its place — no filler stock photography, no generic icon grids.

The menu, story, and location were treated as the three things a visitor actually needs, in that order, with nothing competing for attention.

Design Process

Discover. Design. Develop. Launch.

Discover — a short working session with the owners to understand the in-store experience and translate it into digital cues.

Design — a warm, cream-based palette and an editorial layout tested against real menu content, not lorem ipsum.

Develop — a lightweight, dependency-free build focused on load speed and mobile performance.

Launch — deployed with basic SEO and analytics in place from day one.
